# Chapter 2 — Sāṅkhya Yoga

In this chapter, Śrī Kṛṣṇa begins His direct instruction to Arjuna,
introducing foundational principles regarding the self, duty, action,
and detachment.

The verses in this chapter form the philosophical backbone of the
Bhagavad Gītā.
